M31

Andromeda Galaxy

Also known as the Andromeda Galaxy, M31 was first known to be reported by Abd al-Rahman al-Sufi in The Book of Fixed Stars in 964CE. It is very easily viewed, with an apparent magnitude of 3.1. M31 is the closest galaxy to our own, and it is speculated it is on course to collide with our own Milky Way Galaxy in about 4 billion years.
