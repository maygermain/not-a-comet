M76

Little Dumbbell Nebula

One of four planetary nebulae in the catalogue, M76 was discovered by French astronomer Pierre Méchain in 1780.

M76 can be seen through binoculars in dark skies, but telescopes 8” or larger are needed to resolve its shape. It’s one of the harder Messier objects to spot.
